# Role Onboarding Checklist

Purpose: Help onboard new personas to OctoAcme project processes. This checklist ensures new role occupants know where to find documentation, whom to contact, and what responsibilities they are expected to own.

Before start
- [ ] Read the OctoAcme project management overview and roles documentation
- [ ] Get access to relevant tools (issue tracker, CI, monitoring dashboards)
- [ ] Be added to relevant communication channels (Slack, email lists)

First week
- [ ] Meet with Delivery Lead and Product Owner to understand current priorities
- [ ] Review active releases and upcoming milestones
- [ ] Review the release checklist and any runbooks relevant to the role
- [ ] Identify and meet peers in adjacent roles (Engineering Manager, QA, Security)

First month
- [ ] Shadow at least one release or planning meeting
- [ ] Own a small deliverable or checklist item under supervision
- [ ] Provide feedback on documentation gaps and suggest improvements

Contacts & resources
- Delivery Lead: @delivery-lead
- Product Owner(s): @product-owners
- Release Manager: @release-manager
- QA / Quality Advocate: @quality-advocate
